What is this project?
This is an Android app that helps people understand sign language. It uses the phone's camera to look at hand signs and turns them into text. This helps people who cannot speak to communicate with others easily.

Main Features
Camera Recognition: It looks at hand gestures through the mobile camera.

Easy to Use: The app is designed with a simple and clean look.

A-Z Signs: It can recognize basic sign language alphabets.

Helpful Tool: It is a helpful technology for the deaf and mute community.

Tools Used
Software: Android Studio.

Languages: XML for the design and Java or Kotlin for the code.

How to use it?
Open the app on your Android phone.

Point the camera at a hand gesture.

The app will show the meaning of that sign as text on the screen.
